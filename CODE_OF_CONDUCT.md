# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

GraphRuntime is an open, inclusive project. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to anzize.contact@proton.me. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
